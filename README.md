# Privacy Policy for Orator AI

**Effective Date: January 10, 2025**  
**Last Updated: August 10, 2025**

## Introduction

Welcome to Orator AI ("we", "our", "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring that your personal information is secure. This Privacy Policy outlines our practices regarding the collection, use, and safeguarding of your information when you use our mobile application ("App") available on iOS, Android, and macOS platforms.

## Data Collection

We collect minimal data necessary to provide and improve our services. The data we collect includes:

### 1. Usage Data
- Information about how you use the App, including features accessed and actions taken
- App performance data and crash reports (via Firebase Crashlytics)
- Analytics data to understand user behavior and improve the App (via Firebase Analytics)

### 2. Device Information
- Device type, model, and operating system version
- Unique device identifiers (for app functionality only)
- Language and regional settings

### 3. Account Information
- Phone number (for authentication and account recovery)
- Google account information (if you choose to sign in with Google)
- User preferences and settings

### 4. User-Generated Content
- Audio recordings and transcriptions
- Notes and documents created within the App
- Calendar events and reminders

## Apple App Tracking Transparency (iOS Only)

On iOS devices running iOS 14.5 or later, we request permission to track your activity through Apple's App Tracking Transparency framework. This tracking permission is specifically used for:

- **Apple Search Ads attribution**: To measure the effectiveness of our Apple Search Ads campaigns
- Understanding which ads brought users to our App
- Optimizing our advertising spend on Apple's platform

**Important**: 
- This tracking is solely for Apple Search Ads attribution
- We do not share your data with third-party advertising networks
- You have full control over this permission and can deny or change it at any time in your device's Settings
- If you choose not to allow tracking, you will still have full access to all App features
- Denying tracking permission will not affect your app experience in any way

## Data Storage

### Local Storage
The majority of your data, including recordings, notes, and personal settings, is stored locally on your device. This ensures:
- Your data remains private and under your control
- Faster access to your information
- Functionality even when offline

### Cloud Storage (Firebase Firestore)
Some data is securely stored in the cloud to enable:
- Account synchronization across devices
- Data backup and recovery
- Collaboration features (if applicable)

All cloud-stored data is encrypted both in transit and at rest using industry-standard encryption protocols.

## Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services to enhance App functionality:

### Firebase Services
- **Firebase Authentication**: Secure user authentication
- **Firebase Firestore**: Cloud database for data synchronization
- **Firebase Crashlytics**: Crash reporting and app stability monitoring
- **Firebase Analytics**: Anonymous usage analytics
- **Firebase Cloud Messaging**: Push notifications
- **Firebase Remote Config**: Dynamic app configuration

### Payment Processing
For in-app purchases and subscriptions, we use:
- Apple's In-App Purchase system (iOS/macOS)
- Google Play Billing (Android)

These payment processors handle all payment information securely. We do not store or have access to your payment card details.

### Google Sign-In
Optional authentication method that allows you to sign in using your Google account. Only basic profile information (name and email) is accessed with your consent.

## Data Usage

We use collected data to:
- Provide and maintain App functionality
- Authenticate users and secure accounts
- Improve and optimize App performance
- Send important notifications about your account or App updates
- Provide customer support
- Comply with legal obligations

## Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share data only in the following circumstances:

- **With your consent**: When you explicitly authorize us to share information
- **For legal reasons**: To comply with legal obligations, court orders, or government requests
- **To protect rights**: To protect our rights, property, or safety, or that of our users
- **Service providers**: With trusted third-party service providers who assist us in operating our App (bound by confidentiality agreements)

You have the right to:
- Access your personal information

## Children's Privacy

Our App is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately.

## Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including:
- End-to-end encryption for sensitive data
- Secure HTTPS connections
- Regular security audits and updates

## Advertising and Marketing

### In-App Advertising
Our App **does not display any advertisements** within the application itself. We do not use third-party advertising SDKs to show ads to users.

### Marketing Attribution
We use **Apple Search Ads** to promote our App on the App Store. When you download our App through an Apple Search Ad:
- We may request tracking permission via App Tracking Transparency (ATT) to measure ad campaign effectiveness
- This helps us understand which ads are successful in bringing new users
- The data collected is limited to attribution purposes only
- We do not share this data with third-party advertising networks
- You can opt-out of this tracking at any time without affecting App functionality
